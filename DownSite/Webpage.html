Are you Down?

Down is an application made for the Hackathon event that allows the user to create and join events around their area.

We envision a world where people are free and happy to go outside and experience nature again. In order to achieve that, we created this application that will help communities get together outside of company events and play sports, go hiking, do yoga, or anything else that you may want to do in a group setting outside. We hope that with this encouragement, people will feel safe again.
